This section describes how to delete a PersistentVolumeClaim.

// Warning
include::../../../../_ks_components/admonitions/warning.adoc[]

A PersistentVolumeClaim cannot be recovered after deletion, and by default, the corresponding PersistentVolume will also be deleted. Please perform this operation with caution to avoid data loss.
