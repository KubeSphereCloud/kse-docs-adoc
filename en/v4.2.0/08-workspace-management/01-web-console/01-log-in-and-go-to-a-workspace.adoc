This section describes how to log in to the KubeSphere web console and enter a workspace.


== Prerequisites

* You need to obtain the username and password of a workspace member.

include::../../../_custom/workspaceManagement/webConsole/webConsole-prer-logIn.adoc[]


== Steps

include::../../../_custom/workspaceManagement/webConsole/webConsole-oper-logIn.adoc[]


. Click **Workspace Management** or the **Workspace** icon in the top navigation bar.

. On the **Workspaces** page, click the name of a workspace to enter it.
