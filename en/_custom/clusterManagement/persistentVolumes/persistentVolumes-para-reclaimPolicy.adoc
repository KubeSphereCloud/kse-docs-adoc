Reclaim policy of the persistent volume. The KubeSphere platform uses the **Delete** reclaim policy by default, which means the persistent volume is deleted when the persistent volume claim is deleted. For more information, refer to the link:https://kubernetes.io/zh/docs/concepts/storage/storage-classes/[Kubernetes official documentation].
