This section describes how to delete a constraint.

// Warning
include::../../../../../_ks_components/admonitions/warning.adoc[]

Once a constraint is deleted, it cannot be recovered. Please perform this operation with caution.

include::../../../../../_ks_components/admonitions/admonEnd.adoc[]


== Prerequisites

:relfileprefix: ../../../../
* {empty}
include::../../../../_custom/clusterManagement/clusterManagement-prer-requiredPermission_new.adoc[]

:relfileprefix: ./

* The KubeSphere platform needs to have the **Gatekeeper** extension installed and enabled.


== Steps

include::../../../../_custom/clusterManagement/clusterManagement-oper-openClusterPage.adoc[]

+

include::../../../../../_ks_components/oper-navigate.adoc[]

+

. On the right side of the constraint you want to delete, click image:/images/ks-qkcp/zh/icons/more.svg[more,18,18], and then select **Delete** from the dropdown list.

. In the **Delete Constraint** dialog box, enter the name of the constraint, and then click **OK**.
