== Prerequisites

:relfileprefix: ../../../../

* {empty}
include::../../../../_custom/clusterManagement/clusterManagement-prer-requiredPermission_new.adoc[]

:relfileprefix: ./

* The KubeSphere platform needs to have the **Gatekeeper** extension installed and enabled.

== Steps

include::../../../../_custom/clusterManagement/clusterManagement-oper-openClusterPage.adoc[]

+

include::../../../../../_ks_components/oper-navigate.adoc[]

+

. In the constraint list, click the name of a constraint to open its details page.

. In the **Properties** area of the constraint details page, view the basic information of the constraint.
+
--
[%header,cols="2,5"]
|===
|Parameter |Description

|Cluster
|The cluster to which the constraint belongs.

|Creation Time
|The creation time of the constraint.

|===
--

. In the **Violations** tab of the constraint details page, view violation information.
