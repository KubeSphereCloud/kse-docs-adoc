This section describes how to view platform role details.


== Prerequisites

You need to have thepass:a,q[{ks_permission}] permission on the KubeSphere platform. For more information, refer to link:../../01-users/[Users] and link:../../02-platform-roles/[Platform Roles].

== Steps

include::../../../_custom/platformManagement/platformManagement-oper-logIn.adoc[]
+
. Clickpass:a,q[{ks_menu}].
. In the left navigation pane, select **Platform Roles**.
. In the platform roles list, click the name of a platform role to open its details page.
. On the platform role details page, view the role's creator and creation time in the **Attributes** area.
. On the platform role details page, click the **Permissions** tab to view the permissions held by the current role.
. On the platform role details page, click the **Authorized Users** tab to view users assigned the current platform role.
+
