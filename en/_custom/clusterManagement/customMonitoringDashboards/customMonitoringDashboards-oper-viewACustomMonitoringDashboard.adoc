* On the left side of the page, you can view the monitoring metrics displayed as text.

* In the middle area of the page, you can view line charts or bar charts of the monitoring metrics.

* On the right side of the page, click image:/images/ks-qkcp/zh/icons/chevron-right.svg[chevron-right,18,18] to view the sub-metrics of each monitoring metric, along with their maximum, minimum, average, and last sampled values within the specified time range.

* At the top of the page, click image:/images/ks-qkcp/zh/icons/theme.svg[theme,18,18] to switch between light and dark themes.

* At the top of the page, click image:/images/ks-qkcp/zh/icons/calendar-dark.svg[calendar-dark,18,18] to set the time range for the data.

* At the top of the page, click image:/images/ks-qkcp/zh/icons/refresh-dark.svg[refresh-dark,18,18] to set the refresh interval for the data.

* In the upper right corner of the page, click image:/images/ks-qkcp/zh/icons/close-dark.svg[close,18,18] to return to the custom monitoring dashboard list.
