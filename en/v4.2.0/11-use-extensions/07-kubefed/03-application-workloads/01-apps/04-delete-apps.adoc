This section describes how to delete an app from a federated project.

== Prerequisites

* The **Federated Cluster App Management** extension must be installed and enabled on the KubeSphere platform.

* {empty}
include::../../../../../_custom/multi-clusterProjectManagement/projectManagement-prer-requiredPermission_v4.adoc[]

== Steps

include::../../../../../_custom/multi-clusterProjectManagement/projectManagement-oper-openProjectPage_v4.adoc[]

+

include::../../../../../../_ks_components/oper-navigate.adoc[]

+

. On the **Apps** page, find the app you want to delete, click the image:/images/ks-qkcp/zh/icons/more.svg[more,18,18] icon on the right, and then select **Delete** from the dropdown list.

. In the **Delete App** dialog box, enter the name of the app, and then click **OK**.
+
--
// Warning
include::../../../../../../_ks_components/admonitions/warning.adoc[]

The app cannot be recovered after deletion. Please perform this operation with caution.
