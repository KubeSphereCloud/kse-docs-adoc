This section describes how to edit microservice instance weight.

The weight of a microservice instance is used to determine the traffic distribution for that instance. Traffic sent to the microservice will be allocated to its instances based on their respective weights.


== Prerequisites

* The **KubeSphere Spring Cloud** extension must be installed and enabled on the KubeSphere platform.
