[%header,cols="1a,4a"]
|===
|Parameter |Description

|Events
|
* **PodPending**: Pod is waiting to be created.
* **PodRunning**: Pod is running.
* **PodFailed**: Pod failed to run.
* **PodEvicted**: Pod was evicted.
* **Unknown**: Pod status is unknown.
* **TaskCompleted**: The number of Pods that have successfully completed in the Task has reached the preset completion requirements for that Task.

|Actions

|
* **RestartJob**: Restart the entire Job.
* **RestartTask**: Restart the Task.
* **RestartPod**: Restart the Pod.
* **AbortJob**: Pause the Job; it will not clean up the created Pod resources.
* **TerminateJob**: Terminate the Job and clean up/delete all related Pods.
* **CompleteJob**: Forcefully mark the Job status as "Completed" to end the entire Job.
* **ResumeJob**: Resume a paused Job.
|===
