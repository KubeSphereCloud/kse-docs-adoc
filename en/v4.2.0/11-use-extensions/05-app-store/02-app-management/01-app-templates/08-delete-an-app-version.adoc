This section describes how to delete an app version in an app template.

// Warning
include::../../../../../../_ks_components/admonitions/warning.adoc[]

Once an app version is deleted, it cannot be recovered. Please perform this operation with caution.

include::../../../../../../_ks_components/admonitions/admonEnd.adoc[]


== Prerequisites

:relfileprefix: ../../../../../
* {empty}
include::../../../../../_custom/workspaceManagement/workspaceManagement-prer-requiredPermission_v4.adoc[]

:relfileprefix: ./

* The **KubeSphere App Store Management** extension must be installed and enabled on the KubeSphere platform.

* If the app version you need to delete has been published to the App Store, you need to contact the App Store administrator to unpublish the app version.

+

. On the **Versions** tab of the app template details page, click the app version you want to delete. In the expanded area, click **Delete**, then click **OK** in the pop-up dialog.
