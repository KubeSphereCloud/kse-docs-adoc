This section describes how to delete a log receiver.

== Prerequisites

:relfileprefix: ../../../../../

* {empty}
include::../../../../../_custom/clusterManagement/clusterManagement-prer-requiredPermission_new.adoc[]

:relfileprefix: ./

* A log receiver has been added.

== Steps

include::../../../../../_custom/clusterManagement/clusterManagement-oper-openClusterPage.adoc[]

+

include::../../../../../../_ks_components/oper-navigate.adoc[]

+

. On the **Log Receiver** page, click **Container Logs**, **Resource Events**, **Audit Logs**, or **Notification History**, then click the name of a log receiver in the list to open its details page.

+

. On the log receiver details page, click **Operation > Delete**.

. In the dialog box that appears, enter the name of the log receiver, then click **OK**.
