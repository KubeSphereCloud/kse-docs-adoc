:ks_permission: **Role Management**
:ks_navigation: **Workspace Settings > Workspace Role**

This section describes how to delete a workspace role.

You can delete user-created workspace roles. Built-in workspace roles cannot be deleted.

== Prerequisites

:relfileprefix: ../../../../

* {empty}
include::../../../../_custom/workspaceManagement/workspaceManagement-prer-requiredPermission_v4.adoc[]

:relfileprefix: ./

* If the workspace role you want to delete has already been assigned to workspace members, you need to delete the workspace members or modify their roles.

. In the **Delete Role** dialog box, click **OK**.
