. On the right side of the constraint you want to edit, click image:/images/ks-qkcp/zh/icons/more.svg[more,18,18], and then select **Edit YAML** from the drop-down list.

. In the **Edit YAML** dialog box, modify the relevant configuration, and then click **OK**.
