Upgrade the required extensions in the Extensions Center. For extensions with special configurations that have been backed up, you need to modify the configuration before upgrading.

. In the Extensions Center, click the extension name to enter the extension details page.

. Click image:/images/ks-qkcp/zh/icons/more.svg[more,18,18] under the extension icon and select **Extension Configuration**.

. Clear the **Custom Configuration**, then enter the customized configuration, and click **OK**.

. Click image:/images/ks-qkcp/zh/icons/more.svg[more,18,18] under the extension icon again and select **Update**.

. In the **Extension Update** dialog box, click **Start Update** and wait for the upgrade to complete.

At this point, KubeSphere has completed the entire upgrade process.
