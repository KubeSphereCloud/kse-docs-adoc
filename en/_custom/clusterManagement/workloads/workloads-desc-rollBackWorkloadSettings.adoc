This section describes how to roll back workload settings.

The system will generate revision records after workload creation or modification, which can be used to roll back workload settings. A maximum of 10 revision records can be retained.

// Warning
include::../../../../_ks_components/admonitions/warning.adoc[]

This operation will recreate the workload according to the revision record selected by the user. The business running in the workload will be interrupted. Please perform this operation with caution.
