:ks_permission: **Workspace Settings Management**

This section describes how to delete a workspace.

// Warning
include::../../../../../_ks_components/admonitions/warning.adoc[]

A deleted workspace cannot be recovered. If you choose to also delete the projects within the workspace, all projects and all resources within them will also be deleted. Please perform this operation with caution.

include::../../../../../_ks_components/admonitions/admonEnd.adoc[]

== Steps

include::../../../../_custom/workspaceManagement/workspaceManagement-oper-openWorkspacePage.adoc[]

+

include::../../../../../_ks_components/oper-navigate.adoc[]

+

. In the upper right corner of the **Workspace Information** area, select **Manage > Delete Workspace**.

. In the **Delete Workspace** dialog box, enter the name of the workspace, then click **OK**.
+
--
// Note
include::../../../../../_ks_components/admonitions/note.adoc[]

You can select the **Delete projects in the workspace** checkbox to also delete all projects within the workspace.
