. On the **Resource Status** tab, in the **Containers** section, click image:/images/ks-qkcp/zh/icons/terminal.svg[terminal,18,18] next to the container name to open the container terminal. You can use the container terminal to execute commands inside the container.

. In the upper right corner of the container terminal page, select a container from the dropdown list to quickly access the terminal of other containers within the same pod.
