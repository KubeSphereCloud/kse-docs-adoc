This section describes how to upgrade extensions.

When a new version of an extension is available, you can upgrade it on the extension details page in the Extensions Center.

include::../../_custom/extensionUpgrade/upgradeNote.adoc[]

== Prerequisites

You need to have the **platform-admin** role in the KubeSphere platform. For more information, refer to link:../../05-users-and-roles/01-users/[Users] and link:../../05-users-and-roles/02-platform-roles/[Platform Roles].

== Steps

include::../../_custom/platformManagement/extensionManagement/extensionManagement-oper-logInAsPlatformAdmin.adoc[]

. Click **Extensions Center**.
. Click the name of the installed extension to go to its details page.
. Click image:/images/ks-qkcp/zh/icons/more.svg[more,18,18] under the extension icon and select **Update**.
. In the Update dialog box, follow the extension installation process to complete the upgrade.
