:ks_permission: **Cluster Management**

This section describes how to remove labels from clusters.

== Prerequisites

You need to have the pass:a,q[{ks_permission}] permission on the KubeSphere platform.

== Steps

include::../../../../_custom/platformManagement/platformManagement-oper-logIn.adoc[]

+

. Click **Cluster Management**.

+

include::../../../../_custom/platformManagement/multiclusterManagement/clusterTags/clusterTags-openListPage_v42.adoc[]

+

. To the right of the label you want to remove from clusters, click image:/images/ks-qkcp/zh/icons/more.svg[more,18,18], and then select **Remove from Clusters** from the drop-down list.

. In the **Remove Labels from Clusters** dialog box, to the right of the cluster from which you want to remove the label, click image:/images/ks-qkcp/zh/icons/chain-off-duotone.svg[chain-off-duotone,18,18], and then click **OK**.
