The current status of a volume snapshot.

* **Creating**: The volume snapshot and the corresponding volume snapshot content are being created.

* **Created**: The volume snapshot and the corresponding volume snapshot content have been successfully created.

* **Creation Failed**: An error occurred in the system, and the creation of the volume snapshot or the corresponding volume snapshot content failed.

* **Deleting**: The volume snapshot is being deleted. Depending on the deletion policy of the volume snapshot, the system may retain or automatically delete the corresponding volume snapshot content after the volume snapshot is deleted.
