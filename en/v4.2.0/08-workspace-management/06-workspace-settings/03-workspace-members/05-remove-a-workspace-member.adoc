This section describes how to remove workspace members.

// Note
include::../../../../../_ks_components/admonitions/note.adoc[]

After a user is removed from a workspace, they will lose the permissions granted by the workspace role. If the user has joined projects within the workspace, they will also be removed from those projects.

include::../../../../../_ks_components/admonitions/admonEnd.adoc[]

== Steps

include::../../../../_custom/workspaceManagement/workspaceManagement-oper-openWorkspacePage.adoc[]

+

include::../../../../../_ks_components/oper-navigate.adoc[]

+

. To the right of the workspace member you want to remove, click image:/images/ks-qkcp/zh/icons/more.svg[more,18,18], and then select **Remove**.

. In the **Remove Member** dialog box, enter the username of the workspace member, and then click **OK**.
+
--
The removed workspace member will no longer appear in the workspace member list.
