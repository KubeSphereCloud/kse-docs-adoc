== Delete a Single App

include::../../../../../_ks_components/oper-navigate.adoc[]

include::../../../../_custom/projectManagement/projects-oper-dropdownList.adoc[]

+
. On the **Composed App** page, view the app list.

. To the right of the app you want to delete, click image:/images/ks-qkcp/zh/icons/more.svg[more,18,18], and then select **Delete** from the drop-down list.

. In the **Delete App** dialog box, enter the name of the app, and then click **OK**.

== Delete Apps in Batches

include::../../../../_custom/workspaceManagement/workspaceManagement-oper-openWorkspacePage.adoc[]

+

include::../../../../../_ks_components/oper-navigate.adoc[]

+

include::../../../../_custom/projectManagement/projects-oper-dropdownList.adoc[]

+

. On the **Composed App** page, view the app list.

. Select the checkboxes to the left of the apps you want to delete, and then click **Delete** above the app list.

. In the **Batch Delete Apps** dialog box, enter the names of the apps, and then click **OK**.
